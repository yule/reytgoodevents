Event Description
🎲 THE GREAT YORKSHIRE GEEK MEET 🎲
Roll Dice. Make Friends. Have Fun.
Join us for a welcoming day of board games, roleplaying games, mini painting and social gaming at the Griffin Suite, Shaw Lane Cricket Club in Barnsley.
Whether you're an experienced gamer or you've never rolled a dice before, there's something here for everyone.
Activities Include:
🎲 Board Games
🐉 Dungeons & Dragons
🕰 Blood on the Clocktower
🎨 Mini Painting
🏆 Friendly Tournaments
🛍 Bring & Buy
🍔 Food Available
🍺 Bar Open Throughout The Day
The event is family friendly throughout the day, with social and strategy gaming continuing into the evening.
Tickets
🎟 Adults: £5
👨‍👩‍👧 Under 12s: FREE
All children must be accompanied by an adult.
Advance booking will provide early access to reserve places on selected Dungeons & Dragons and Blood on the Clocktower sessions.
Accessibility
♿ Excellent disabled access.
🚗 Free parking available on site.
Everyone is welcome — beginners, families, friends, experienced gamers and anyone curious about tabletop gaming.
