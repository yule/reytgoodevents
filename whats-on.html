What's On

A full day of tabletop gaming, painting and social fun — {{ site.event.date_display }}, {{ site.event.time_display }}.

🎲

Board Games

Drop in and play a game with friends, or pull up a chair at a table of new faces. Bring your own favourites or try something from the games library on the day.

🐉

Dungeons & Dragons

Join a one-shot adventure run by an experienced game master — no character or experience required, just turn up ready to roll. Advance booking gives early access to reserve a place.

🕰

Blood on the Clocktower

A social deduction game of secrets, bluffing and betrayal for a table of townsfolk. Sessions are limited, so book in advance to reserve your seat.

🎨

Mini Painting

Sit down and paint a miniature at your own pace. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned painter, there's space and help on hand.

🏆

Friendly Tournaments

Casual, welcoming competitive play throughout the day. Come for the challenge, stay for the company — all skill levels welcome.

🛍

Bring & Buy

Buy, sell and trade board games, miniatures and gaming gear with other attendees. A great spot to find your next favourite game.

🍔

Food Available

Food will be available on site throughout the event, so you won't need to leave to refuel between games.

🍺

Bar Open Throughout The Day

The bar is open all day and into the evening, as social and strategy gaming continues after the family-friendly session winds down.

Booking D&D or Blood on the Clocktower?

Advance booking provides early access to reserve places on selected Dungeons & Dragons and Blood on the Clocktower sessions, as spaces are limited.
